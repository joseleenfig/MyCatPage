9 Lives

Cats have many Super natural powers that many aren't aware of. As a former dog person, I rescued a cat and ever since I have been
a cat lover. Cat's connection to the paranormal world stems from their senses and influencing the electromagnetic fields. Cat's are also known for being able
to tap into other dimensions. Not only so, they have great night visions,hunting skills, reflexes, and many say they have 9 lives.

Cat's are also know to be superior in Egypt history.
Cats in Egypt were very popular due to their jobs cleaning out and protecting grain. The Cat's in Egypt had a very important job, being that grain was their main
source of food. Egyptian cats were called Mau, not only did they believe they were important for protecting their crops, but also protectors from bad energy.
